# Goblin

Small green creatures with a nasty attitude.

## Goblin Hierarchy

Goblin society is rigidly hierarchical, with strength determining rank.

### Common Tier
- **Grunt** — Level 1-5
- **Warrior** — Level 5-10
- **Veteran** — Level 10-15

### Elite Tier
- **Commander** — Level 15-20

### Boss Tier
- **Warlord** — Level 20+ (e.g., [Eron Vosk](../characters/villains/eron-vosk.md) at Level 25)

## Stat Modifiers

| Stat | Modifier |
|------|----------|
| Strength | -1 |
| Dexterity | +2 |
| Constitution | -1 |
| Intelligence | +1 |
| Wisdom | -1 |
| Charisma | -2 |

## Racial Traits

- **Small Size** — Easier to hide, harder to hit, reduced carry capacity
- **Darkvision** — Can see in darkness up to 60 feet
- **Pack Tactics** — Advantage when allies are adjacent to target

## Notable Goblins

- [Eron Vosk](../characters/villains/eron-vosk.md) — Level 25 Goblin Warlord, Mind Mage
