# IQuantum

A Quantum Computer company that builds and experiments on new Quantum Computing technology.

The organization maintains both an R&D division and sells stable machines to academic and government buyers.

## Significance

IQuantum is the location where the actual simulation is running in the "real world."

## Location

Saratoga, California ([San Francisco Bay Area](../locations/bay-area.md))
