# Magic

Magic fundamentally involves channeling mana to alter one's surroundings. In magical worlds, mana is everywhere, like air.

## Schools of Magic

There are eight major magic categories:

1. **[System](schools/system-magic.md)** — Manipulation of the underlying system/simulation
2. **Healing** — Restoration and divine power
3. **Temporal** — Time manipulation
4. **Elemental** — Fire, water, earth, air
5. **Mind** — Charm, control, telepathy
6. **Force** — Kinetic and physical manipulation
7. **Gravity** — Weight and attraction manipulation
8. **Metamagic** — Magic that modifies other magic

## Common Beginner Spells

These spells are accessible to most magic users:

- [Ignite](spells/ignite.md) — Create a small flame
- [Create Light](spells/create-light.md) — Generate a light source
- [Create Water](spells/create-water.md) — Conjure a small amount of water
